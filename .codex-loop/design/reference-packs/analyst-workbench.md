# Reference Pack: analyst-workbench

Use this when people need to compare scenarios, inspect traces, review structured findings, or work through evidence step by step.

## Core feel

- analytical, methodical, and slightly academic
- multiple related surfaces that stay aligned under heavy information load
- strong table rhythm and note hierarchy
- quiet interface that rewards careful reading

## Structural cues

- comparison tables first, cards second
- reserve accent color for status change and orientation
- support long-form notes beside structured data
- surface assumptions, caveats, and reviewer notes without clutter

## Good fits

- incident review tools
- research and evaluation dashboards
- policy comparison views
- review queues and human-in-the-loop systems

## Avoid

- ornamental sidebars
- highly animated widgets
- oversized empty whitespace between related facts
- vague summary tiles with no drill-down path
