# Reference Pack: citizen-service

Use this when the product serves ordinary citizens, families, or public-service users who need clarity, reassurance, and easy-to-follow steps.

## Core feel

- warm but disciplined
- plain-language explanations supported by clear structure
- trust comes from predictability, not from cute visuals
- accessible flow and obvious next actions

## Structural cues

- keep the main action path visible at all times
- explain requirements, approvals, and outcomes in short blocks
- use tables for eligibility, fees, deadlines, and process comparisons
- when visual cues are used, they should clarify task progress or responsibility

## Good fits

- public-service assistants
- citizen-facing delegation flows
- booking and application helpers
- guardian or caregiver approval workflows

## Avoid

- futuristic AI motifs
- dense jargon without interpretation
- unclear ownership of decisions or payments
- decorative illustrations that do not help completion
