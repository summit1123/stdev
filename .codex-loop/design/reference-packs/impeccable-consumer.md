# Reference Pack: impeccable-consumer

Inspired by `impeccable`, use this when the product is consumer-facing and must feel polished, clear, and human without drifting into templated AI startup styling.

## Core feel

- calm, intentional, and legible
- copy is short and useful, not explanatory for its own sake
- hierarchy is obvious on first glance
- polish is felt through rhythm and clarity more than decoration

## Structural cues

- keep one clear primary action per surface
- support actions with concise guidance nearby, not in detached callout boxes
- use color deliberately to direct action or signal trust, never as wallpaper
- ensure states and consequences are visible before commitment
- make mobile layouts feel first-class, not compressed desktop ports

## Anti-patterns to avoid

- generic SaaS landing-page cards inside product screens
- too many accent colors competing for attention
- text-heavy empty states that describe the interface instead of helping completion
- huge hero areas in workflow screens
- AI-themed gradients, sparkles, or mascot-like decoration

## Good fits

- booking flows
- scheduling and coordination tools
- personal productivity assistants
- family or guardian approval experiences
